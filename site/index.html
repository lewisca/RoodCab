How it works

A loop with a verifier. The brain proposes a referral; a second pass confirms it's real, fresh, non-duplicate, eligible, and compliant before the hand ever moves.

  1. 1

    Connect

    Your CRM sends a "New Report Imported" event through Zapier to a secure webhook. Transport only — it carries scores, never detects crossings.

  2. 2

    Detect

    Rood Cab takes the median of the three bureau scores and checks it against the client's last reading to spot an upward band crossing.

  3. 3

    Verify

    A second agent runs six gates — score sanity, real crossing, idempotency, eligibility, consent, and an independent re-derivation. Any doubt → quarantine.

  4. 4

    Refer

    One matched offer for that tier goes out with a tracked link. A multi-tier jump routes to the highest band reached — still just one referral.

  5. 5

    Earn

    You collect commission on conversion. Every event is logged with a unique sub-id so payouts reconcile — and no client PII is ever stored.

Compliance is the product

A wrongful offer can cost far more than a missed one. Rood Cab is built so the safe choice is the default — when anything is uncertain, it quarantines for human review instead of sending.

FCRA

Never pulls or stores credit-report line items, tradelines, or dispute data. Scores, contact, and consent only.

TCPA

Consent to lending-offer referrals lives in your client agreement — documented and auditable, never inferred.

RESPA

The mortgage tier is routed through a licensed partner or bona-fide marketing fee — never a per-referral kickback.

CROA

Message copy makes no credit-outcome promises or guarantees. Plain, honest nudges only.

No PII at rest

Memory keeps client IDs, scores, bands, and attribution keys — no names, emails, phones, SSNs, or addresses.

Idempotent

One referral per client per band, ever. Replays and duplicate webhooks resolve to no-action, not noise.
